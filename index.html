About Me


I am Md. Amanullah Khandkar,now the owner of "Mugal Food Network". But this shop is previously known as" Mughal Biriani Ghar". My ancestors were also involved in this business. I learned the recipies from my father and grandfather. I am glad to say that i retained the taste of the original recipies. Although many modern recipies also sold in our shop but my main focus is in the heritage food items of Dhaka.
